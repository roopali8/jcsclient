NAME:
    update-role


DESCRIPTION:
    Updates a role for your JCS account.


SYNOPSIS:
    update-role [--name <value>]  [--id <value>]  --assume-role-policy-document <value>


OPTIONS:
    --name (string)
    The name of the role to update.
    This parameter is optional.

    --id (string)
    The id of the role to update
    This parameter is optional. Either of id or name is required

    --assume-role-policy-document (string)
    The assume role policy document of the role.


EXAMPLES:
    jcs iam update-user --name user2 --assume-role-policy-document {"statement":[{"effect”:”deny”,”principal":{"jcs":["jrn:jcs:iam:665137897895"]},"action":["jrn:jcs:iam:AssumeRole"]}]}
    jcs iam update-user --id 10001111911 --assume-role-policy-document {"statement":[{"effect”:”deny”,”principal":{"jcs":["jrn:jcs:iam:665137897895"]},"action":["jrn:jcs:iam:AssumeRole"]}]}
