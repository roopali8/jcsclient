NAME:
    delete-role


DESCRIPTION:
    Deletes the role of your JCS account.


SYNOPSIS:
    delete-role [ --id <value>]  [--name <value>]


OPTIONS:
    --id (string)
    The id of the role to delete.
    This parameter is optional.

    --name (string)
    The name of the role to delete.
    This parameter is optional. Either of id or name is required


EXAMPLES:
    jcs iam delete-role --id 10011111100111
    jcs iam delete-role --name role111
