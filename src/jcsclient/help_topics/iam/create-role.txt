NAME:
    create-role


DESCRIPTION:
    Creates a new role for your JCS account.


SYNOPSIS:
    create-role --name <value> --assume-role-policy-document <value>


OPTIONS:
    --name (string)
    The name of the role to create.

    --assume-role-policy-document (JSON)
    The assume role policy document of the role.


EXAMPLES:
    jcs iam create-user --name user2 --assume-role-policy-document  {"statement":[{"effect":"allow","principal":{"jcs":["jrn:jcs:iam:665137897895"]},"action":["jrn:jcs:iam:AssumeRole"]}]}
