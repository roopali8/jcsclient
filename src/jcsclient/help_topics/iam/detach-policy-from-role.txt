NAME:
    detach-policy-from-role


DESCRIPTION:
    Detached policy from role for your JCS account.


SYNOPSIS:
    detach-policy-from-role  [ --role-name <value>]  [ --policy-name <value>]  [ --role-id <value>]  [ --policy-id <value>]


OPTIONS:
    --role-name (string)
    The name of the role the policy is detached to.
    This parameter is optional.

    --role-id (string)
    The id of the role the policy is detached to.
    This parameter is optional. Either of role-id or role-name is required.

    --policy-name(string)
    The name of the policy needs to detached to role.
    This parameter is optional.

    --policy-id (string)
    The id of the policy needs to detached to role.
    This parameter is optional. Either of policy-id or policy-name is required.


EXAMPLES:
    jcs iam detach-policy-from-role --role-name role111 --policy-name policy111
    jcs iam detach-policy-from-role --role-id 102913813 --policy-name policy111
    jcs iam detach-policy-from-role --role-name role111 --policy-id 10391232180
    jcs iam detach-policy-from-role --role-id 102913813 --policy-id 10391232180
